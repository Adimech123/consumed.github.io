About the Artist

I'm a designer and artist with a curious mind and a love for bold ideas. My practice blends digital and physical mediums. From digital design and visual identities to paintings and interactive installations. I’m drawn to work that tells stories, invites interaction, and sparks a sense of wonder. Whether it’s crafting a vibrant visual experience or experimenting with new materials, I approach each project with creativity, care, and a bit of fun..

Inspired by both personal and shared experiences, my work explores how we connect with objects and how time shapes those relationships. I create detailed, thoughtful pieces that invite viewers to pause, reflect, and consider the stories that everyday things can hold.
