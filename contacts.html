About the Artist

Amelie Dimech is a textile artist specializing in contemporary tapestries that explore themes of consumption, memory, and material culture. Her work has been exhibited in galleries across Europe and North America.

Drawing inspiration from personal experiences and cultural observations, Amelie creates intricate pieces that invite viewers to reflect on their relationship with objects and consumption patterns in modern society.

Amelie holds a Master of Fine Arts degree and has been creating her distinctive tapestries for over a decade. She conducts workshops and speaking engagements on textile art and sustainable creative practices.
